About me

Omoniyi Balogun is an Industrial and organisational Psychologist. As an expert in the study of human behavior, Omoniyi has extensive organizational behavior experience. He has cognate experience, working as a radio presenter for Workplace Psychology on Lion FM University of Nigeria Nsukka and Nigerian Television Authority(NTA) Enugu. Currently Omoniyi works with Emzor pharmaceutical Industries Limited, one of the leading indigenous pharmaceutical companies in Africa, as the companyâ€™s corporate behavior expert.

He is responsible for Relationship management, the resolution of customer issues and complaints, conducting research and conduct personality assessments for staff. He is an Associate Member of The Chartered Institute of Personnel Management of Nigeria and also an Alumni of Daystar Leadership Academy(DLA). Omoniyi had worked in the Human Resources Department at Emzor pharmaceutical Industries Ltd, Customer service unit and currently working as personal assistant to the Managing director. He has more than two years teaching experience with the University of Ibadan Distance Learning Lagos centre as a lead tutor, where he teaches introduction to psychology, personnel and industrial psychology, psychological testing & test construction, leadership style and motivation.
